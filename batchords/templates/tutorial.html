Tutorial

Getting set-up

  • Plug in the Akai Keyboard to your computer.
  • In another window or tab, navigate to localhost:8000

Available Tools

  • Press any combination of keys on the Akai keyboard and watch them light up in blue on the keyboard graphic at the bottom of the page
  • Press the Start button to begin playback of the score.
  • Press the Stop button to pause playback of the score.
  • Press "Seek Note Right" to move to the cursor to the next note in the measure, and press "Seek Measure Right" to move to the next measure.
  • Press "Seek Note Left" to move to the cursor to the previous note in the measure, and press "Seek Measure Left" to move to the previous measure.
